Golf Solitaire with text art

Try to clear the table of cards by putting the bottom cards from the table in the waste deck (It's the bottom deck with the face up). You can click the sellection deck (the one with the face down) to put a random card in the waste deck. If you run out of cards in the selecton deck you lose.

Restart the game by refreshing the page.
